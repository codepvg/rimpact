About RIMPACT

Fine Arts

RIMPACT looks forward to lets every artist from PVG's CoET explore their interest and abilities in field of fine arts, including sketches, paintings, crafts, sand arts designs, graphics, and many more artforms and provide them with a platform to showcase their skills.

Performing Arts

RIMPACT also gives a great stage to the amazing performers at PVG's CoET and helps them discover the hidden talent in themselves, in fields like singing, dancing, instrumental music, short films, skits, stand up comedies, and a lot of different kinds of performances.

Digital Arts

In the era of Metaverse and Web3 RIMPACT also gives the platform to the people having phenomenal expertise in digital arts, NFTs and their even more digital creations!
